Egg Fried Rice

Egg Fried Rice Photo by, Wally Gobetz from Flickr, licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 2.0.

Description

A 10 minutes egg fried rice recipe from Christie At Home.

Ingredients

  • Day-Old Cold Cooked Jasmine Rice
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan, pour in beaten eggs and quickly stir-fry the eggs
  2. Once the eggs take shape but are still moist, quickly toss in rice. If rice is clumped together, break apart in pan.
  3. Sweep everything to the side of the pan, add remaining oil into empty space with green onions.
  4. Mix green onions into rice and eggs.
  5. Add soy sauce and sesame oil along the edges of the pan. Then toss or mix until grains are colored brown.
  6. Remove off heat. Serve hot and enjoy while it's hot!
